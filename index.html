Apollonia is building the operating system for how dentists see, record, and understand their work. From a loupe-mounted camera to full augmented reality.

01 · Product Roadmap
Four phases to the full vision
Each phase is commercially viable on its own while building the infrastructure, data moat, and market positioning required for the next.
Phase 1 Now · H2 2026 ● Live

Apollonia Camera

The world's first purpose-built clinical camera for dental professionals. Mounts to existing loupes or safety glasses. Captures first-person 4K video with zero workflow disruption.

◇

38g Ultra-Light

Lighter than a AAA battery. Mounts to any existing loupes or frames without fatigue.

◎

4K @ 30fps

Clinical-grade video with enough resolution for diagnostics, case review, and patient education.

☼

Built-in LED

Integrated illumination ensures consistent lighting in the oral cavity without external light sources.

⊘

Hands-Free Control

Foot pedal operation keeps clinical workflow sterile and uninterrupted. Start, stop, snapshot. No hands required.

▣

Live Display

Real-time feed to a chairside display for patient communication and team visibility during procedures.

⊙

POV Capture

First-person perspective means the camera sees exactly what the clinician sees. True clinical documentation.

Phase 2 H2 2026 · H1 2027 ● Next

Apollonia Studio + AI

A browser-based cloud platform that turns raw clinical footage into structured, searchable, AI-enriched content. Voice commands on the hardware begin transforming the camera from a recording device into an intelligent clinical assistant.

△

Cloud Storage

Secure, HIPAA-aligned clinical video storage with automatic sync from device. No more SD cards or manual file management.

⧫

Native Video Editing

In-browser trimming, splicing, annotation, and export. Purpose-built for clinical workflows, not repurposed consumer tools.

◈

Voice Commands

Hardware-level voice control. "Apollonia, start recording", "take photo", "bookmark". Hands and feet stay free.

⬡

AI Contextual Awareness

The camera begins to understand what it's seeing. Procedure recognition, auto-tagging, and intelligent capture go beyond passive recording.

▤

Dental Scribe Integration

AI-powered clinical documentation that generates notes from video and voice. The beginning of automated charting.

↗

Export & Share

One-click export for patient education, referral letters, insurance claims, and social media content creation.

Direct connection to practice management software. Patient history, charting, and notes accessible without leaving the chair.

02 · Strategic Moat
Why this compounds
Each phase creates defensibility that makes the next phase harder to replicate. By Phase 4, the moat is structural.
△

Clinical Data Flywheel

Every recording feeds the AI. More clinicians → more data → better AI → more clinicians. The compounding data advantage starts in Phase 1 and accelerates through every subsequent phase.

◆

Workflow Lock-In

Apollonia Studio becomes the operating system for clinical documentation. Once a practice's entire video library, AI-generated notes, and patient records live in the platform, switching costs are enormous.

▣

Hardware + Software Vertical

Owning both the capture device and the software platform creates an integrated experience that horizontal competitors can't match. Apple-like control over the full stack.

⊘

Patent Portfolio

Filed patents in Australia and China protect core innovations in loupe-mounted clinical capture and AR-integrated dental workflows. IP moat deepens with each phase.

◇

Category Creation

Apollonia is not entering an existing category. It is creating one. Being first is not enough; being known as first is. Every phase reinforces Apollonia as the category king of intelligent clinical vision.

⊙

Clinician Community

Beta testers become advocates. Early adopters shape the product and evangelize it. By Phase 3, Apollonia has a community of clinicians co-building the future of their profession.

03 · Revenue Architecture
Layered revenue that grows with each phase
Hardware margins fund software development. Software creates recurring revenue. Each phase adds a new revenue layer without cannibalising the last.
Apollonia Camera Hardware
Device sales + accessories. Foundation revenue from Phase 1 onwards.
Apollonia Studio SaaS
Monthly subscription for cloud storage, AI features, and video editing platform.
Apollonia Loupes Hardware
Premium AR-enabled loupes. Higher ASP than camera. Recurring lens/frame upgrades.
Apollonia Vision Hardware + Platform
Full AR glasses + premium platform tier. Highest margin product with strongest lock-in.
